# About Me

In my spare time I enjoy reading, programming and messing around with linux. I'm also a big fan of [Vinland Saga](https://www.imdb.com/title/tt10233448) & [The Binding of Issac](https://www.igdb.com/games/the-binding-of-isaac-repentance).

# My Setup
![](/images/setup.webp)

I do everything on my Thinkpad T14 laptop running [Hyprland](https://hypr.land/) ([DMS](https://danklinux.com/)) on [CachyOS](https://cachyos.org/). For my code editor, I am right now using [Zed](https://zed.dev/) with its AI features turned off, as I find it to be much faster than editors like [VS Code](https://code.visualstudio.com/).
